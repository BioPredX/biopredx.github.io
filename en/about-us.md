# About us

BioPredX is a computational biology company developing predictive technologies for protein engineering and genomic interpretation. Founded as a spin-off from the University of Zaragoza, we combine structural biology, machine learning and advanced data analytics to translate complex molecular information into practical solutions for biotechnology and biomedicine.

Our technologies empower researchers, biotech companies and healthcare professionals to make faster, better-informed decisions, accelerating protein optimisation and supporting the interpretation of human genetic variation.

We are certain predictive biology will become one of the fundamental drivers of the next generation of biotechnology and precision medicine. By anticipating molecular behaviour through data, modelling, and mechanistic  understanding, we fast-track the progress of new technologies and processes.

## El equipo
